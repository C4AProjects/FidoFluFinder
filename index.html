ABOUT US:
Fido Flu Finder

FidoFluFinder was founded by biosurveillance professionals concerned about the lack of a companion animal surveillance system, particularly in light of the current canine influenza situation. The current site is designed to meet the immediate need of providing easily accessible information regarding the extent of canine influenza in the U.S. We anticipate that this will expand and evolve over time.
